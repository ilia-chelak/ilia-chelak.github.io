Computer Vision PhD Student in the Sato lab at UTokyo.

I am co-supervised by Professors Yoichi Sato (UTokyo) and Yasuyuki Matsushita (Microsoft Research). I am interested in solving 3D Computer Vision problems using Deep Learning. My current research focus is real-time 3D reconstruction from monocular video streams. Previously, I was a lead engineer working on invisible image watermarking at Watermarked.io. I have also engaged in computer vision research at UHelsinki, UOsaka, and LUT.

News

04/2025: I joined Prof. Yoichi Sato's lab at the University of Tokyo as a PhD student. I will be co-supervised by Prof. Yasuyuki Matsushita.
05/2024: I have graduated from the University of Helsinki! My thesis, centered on multi-view 3D reconstruction, sped up convergence by 4x (to 7 minutes down from 30) while greatly improving the geometry.
10/2023: I joined Matsushita group at Osaka University for a 6-month research exchange.
